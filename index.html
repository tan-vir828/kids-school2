Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The standard curriculum for ages 3-6 fosters language, numeracy, motor, and social skills through play-based learning, promoting creativity, curiosity, and emotional development in early learners.

Elementary School

The elementary school curriculum emphasizes literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and foundational knowledge essential for academic and personal growth.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, subject mastery, teamwork, and problem-solving through diverse academic and extracurricular activities, preparing students for higher education and life challenges.
